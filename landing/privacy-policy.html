Privacy Policy

Privacy in plain language.

Effective date: April 15, 2026

1. Scope

This policy explains how Ocean Drops collects, uses, and protects data when you use the app and related services.

2. Data we may collect

  • Account details such as email address and sign-in provider.
  • User content including notes, reminders, checklists, links, files, images, and location entries you choose to store.
  • Collaboration data such as shared folders, invitations, and member access details.
  • Basic technical and usage metadata such as timestamps, device type, language preference, and analytics events.

3. How we use data

  • To provide core app functionality and synchronization.
  • To support reminders, sharing, and collaboration features.
  • To improve reliability, performance, and product quality.
  • To detect abuse, protect service integrity, and prevent fraud.

4. Infrastructure and storage

Ocean Drops may use managed cloud services, including Firebase products such as Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage, and Cloud Functions, to provide secure account and content operations.

5. Security practices

We apply access controls, authentication checks, and security rules designed to reduce unauthorized access. No system is perfectly secure, but safeguards are continuously improved.

6. Sharing and disclosure

Your content is not sold. Data may be shared only when required to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, enforce platform protections, or complete features you choose to use.

7. Your rights and choices

  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of data.
  • You can remove shared access and revoke invitations.
  • You can contact us for privacy requests, account issues, or deletion support.

8. Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for service delivery, security, compliance, and legitimate operational needs. When data is no longer required, we aim to delete or anonymize it.

9. Children

Ocean Drops is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us and we will investigate.

10. Policy updates

We may update this policy to reflect product or legal changes. Material updates will be reflected by a revised effective date.

11. Privacy contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact: privacy@oceandrops.app
